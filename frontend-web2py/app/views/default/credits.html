ACKNOWLEDGMENTS

If you have used the Hypatia Catalog in published research, please include the following acknowledgement:

The research shown here acknowledges use of the Hypatia Catalog, an online compilation of stellar abundance data as described in Hinkel et al. (2014, AJ, 148, 54).

Additionally, please cite the original Hypatia Catalog paper: https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2014AJ....148...54H/.

In the event that a small number of catalogs was used within an analysis, we highly encourage the citation of those individual papers. However, we still appreciate the acknowledgment of the Hypatia Catalog and Database, in order to give credit for the use of the website, internal algorithms, and availability of the data (since >50% of the catalogs were not available in machine-readable formats prior to this compilation).

Dr. Natalie Hinkel originally created the Hypatia Catalog and designed the science-data pipeline. She actively maintains the data and data compilation.

Dr. Caleb Wheeler is the developer for the Hypatia Catalog website, database, API, and science-data pipeline.

The original developer for the online Hypatia Catalog was Dan Burger, who was supported by Nitin Pasumarthy, Benjamin Knight, and Wendy Yu.

Logo by Nahks Tr’Ehnl, who can be found at www.nahks.com.

Additional thanks to Dr. Garrett Somers.

Please send any website or data updates, issues, or corrections to hypatiacatalog@gmail.com. You can also find us on Twitter via @hypatiacatalog.
